The Unseen University library
============================================

.. image:: /_static/images/librarian.png
  :alt: UU Librarian.

In Ankh-Morpork, the Unseen University library is the strongest point of l-space on the Disc. All libraries connect
through it. All knowledge that was ever written, and some that has not been written yet, is accessible to those who
know how to navigate the stacks. The Librarian, who is an orangutan and would prefer not to be reminded otherwise,
tends the place with quiet authority.
