State visibility: a surveillance threat model
==============================================

A greenhouse was built to be seen into. The question is who designed it, who holds the keys,
and whether the plants inside ever agreed to be on display. This model maps how nation-state
surveillance operates in the EU context: the legal framework that permits it, the commercial
infrastructure that extends it, and the layered consequences for citizens, civil society,
companies, and the EU itself.
